About The Couple

A tale of patience and pursuit...

Eric mustered up the nerve to contact Jackie after seeing her profile online. A Chicago girl that worked at Google couldn't be all that bad. Alas, there was no response. But months later... Jackie finally replied! After some back and forth, the two set up a date at Google's local watering hole, The Sports Page. They hit it off right away. Eric's jokes went incredibly well (Jackie laughed at everything), but it was their Midwest connection that brought the chemistry. No one understands tornadoes, snow, and corn like the Heartland.

Enter the second date. All in all, it was pretty good! Sushi was consumed, delicious cocktails were had. Eric thought things were off to an amazing start. Then, radio silence. Apparently, Jackie wasn't interested. Come on! After 4 months of showing extreme patience and several attempts to hang out again, Eric was ready to give up on her. But, he decided to give it one last shot. Google's holiday party was right around the corner and asked her to be his date.

That was the first time Jackie said "Yes". It wouldn't be the last.

The two became inseparable after the holidays. Jackie's roommate started asking her, "So...who's this Chrome Guy?!". There was lots of golf outings, lots of movies (a love of Jackie's), and lots of attempts at cooking extravagant meals. Some of them were even successful.

Fast forward to today...Jackie and Eric are cooking for each other all the time. They've shared laughs, smiles, and tons of burritos together. Eric is even best friends with Pattie! They're on a mission to travel to world together, and are off to a good start by visiting Amsterdam, India, Greece, France, and Italyâ€¦ just to name a few!

Eric and Jackie have amazing friends and family, and grateful to see their circles grow stronger together.
